About Us

We are the NSBM Table Tennis Club – a community of passionate players who strive to improve skills, promote sportsmanship, and represent NSBM in tournaments with pride. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced player, our club welcomes you.

Our Vision

To be recognized as one of the leading university sports clubs, nurturing talented players while fostering teamwork, discipline, and a love for the game of table tennis.
