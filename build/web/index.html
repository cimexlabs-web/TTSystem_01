About Us

The NSBM Table Tennis Club is a vibrant community of table tennis enthusiasts at NSBM Green University. We welcome players of all skill levels, from beginners to advanced competitors.

Founded in 2018, our club has grown to over 200 active members who participate in regular training sessions, friendly matches, and competitive tournaments.

We believe in fostering sportsmanship, friendship, and a love for the game of table tennis. Our certified coaches provide training to help members improve their skills and reach their full potential.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the leading university table tennis club in Sri Lanka, recognized for excellence in sportsmanship, player development, and community engagement.

We aim to:

  • Promote table tennis as a sport for all students
  • Develop competitive players who can represent NSBM at national and international levels
  • Create a welcoming community that fosters friendship and personal growth
  • Organize tournaments and events that bring the table tennis community together
